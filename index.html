Introduction

Hey! I'm Max, a Full Stack Web Developer based in Edinburgh, Scotland. I'm focused on designing and developing beautiful, functional and user-friendly websites.

My background is in marketing and graphic design and so this experience allows me to approach each project with the bigger strategic picture in mind and a well defined design idea.

I'm constantly looking to improve on my coding skills, to learn new languages and frameworks, and I consider it imperative to keep up with industry trends and advancements.

After recently completing a two year course in Software Development I'm now ready to take on a Junior Web Developer role. As a freelancer, I'm also currently open to new web projects, so if you have a requirement for a bespoke website then I would be happy to hear from you!

Keep scrolling to find out more about my technical skills and to see my project portfolio thus far. And if you have a particular project in mind and would like to discuss the requirements you can use the contact form at the bottom of the page to get in touch with me.

Alternatively feel free to email me directly at max_miguta@yahoo.com
